2015

At the beginning of 2014 I did what most people do -- I set up some new years resolutions and some goals to go along with them. About a month later I had forgotten about them or gave up on most of them entirely.

I made this page in an effort to make 2015 different from 2014. Here's a list of all my goals and my progress on all of them.

This year's goals

1. Run 400 miles

Starting off my first goal by setting the bar low. This comes out to a 2.5 mile run 3 times a week. This is not overwhelming and I can still probably finish it even if I get off to a bad start.

2. Go to the gym 150 times

I like the 3 times a week rule. This, along with the running, gives me something active to do almost every day of the week with one day of rest.

3. Go to yoga 50 times

This one is a really low bar and I'm hoping to clear it with room to spare. This is only once a week and leaves two weeks for vacation or just being busy.

4. Read 25 books

This comes out to about 2 a month. In 2014 I read more than this, but I was definitely rushing to hit my goal. This year I'm setting a lower bar so that I actually enjoy what I'm reading.
